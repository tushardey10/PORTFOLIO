About Me

I am a dedicated PhD student and researcher in the field of condensed matter and materials physics, with a focus on strongly correlated systems. With a strong foundation in physics and computational methods, I am passionate about understanding complex quantum phenomena and their applications in superconductivity and novel materials.

My research interests include excitonic insulator states in topological materials, strongly correlated fermions, and 2D van der Waals heterostructures. I utilize computational tools like Python and Mathematica to model and analyze these complex physical systems.
